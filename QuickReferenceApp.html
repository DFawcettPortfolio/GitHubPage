Quick Reference App

5e Quick Reference App for the SRD:

*** THIS APP IS STILL UNDER ACTIVE DEVELOPMENT, PLEASE TREAT THE CURRENT STATE AS A WORK IN PROGRESS AND CHECK BACK LATER TO SEE MORE! ***

A companion tool for D&D 5e (2014), built in Android Studio using Kotlin, designed for quick and intuitive rules lookup during gameplay. The app organizes official SRD 5.1 content into an easy-to-navigate interface and provides tools for managing creatures and rules at the table.

Key Features:
  • Favorites Page: Save frequently used stat blocks for instant access.
  • Menagerie Tracker: Track current HP for summons, mounts, pets, and companions in one convenient place.
  • Comprehensive SRD Content: All SRD stat blocks, sorted by creature type, plus all spells that summon or reference creatures linked directly to their details.
  • Condition & Rules Guide: Quick reference for conditions like Grappled and Incapacitated, along with other commonly consulted rules.
